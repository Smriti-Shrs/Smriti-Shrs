## About Me

I am **Smriti Shrestha**, a **Final Year BIT Student** from Nepal building practical, responsive, and user-friendly web experiences.

My work focuses on **WordPress development**, **Laravel development**, **frontend development**, **React.js**, and **SEO optimization**. I enjoy creating websites and systems that are clean, easy to use, performance-aware, and ready for real users, clients, and businesses.

Currently, I am building projects, improving my development workflow, and growing as a full-stack web developer with a strong focus on polished digital experiences.
